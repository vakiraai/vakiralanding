## What is Vakira.AI?

Vakira.AI is an AI-powered English voice communication coaching platform built specifically for Indian professionals and students. It provides real-time feedback on spoken English, grammar, pronunciation, and communication skills.

## Core Problem We Solve

Many Indian professionals are highly skilled but struggle with English communication — freezing in meetings, underperforming in interviews, or feeling judged when speaking. Traditional English classes teach grammar rules but never give real speaking practice. Vakira.AI fills this gap with a judgment-free AI coach available 24/7.

## Target Audience

- Indian working professionals (IT, banking, MBA, marketing)
- Students preparing for job interviews
- Anyone who wants to improve spoken English confidence
- People learning to communicate in professional English environments

## Key Features

1. **AI Voice Conversation** — Real spoken conversations with AI on any topic. Instant feedback on grammar, fluency, and word choice.
2. **Interview Preparation** — Mock interviews for IT, banking, MBA roles. Scored on content, confidence, grammar, and delivery.
3. **Grammar Coach** — Interactive exercises targeting weak areas. Explanations available in English or Hindi.
4. **Pronunciation Training** — Word-level accuracy scoring. Identifies specific errors and how to fix them.
5. **Progress Reports** — Fluency scores, grammar accuracy, daily streaks. Downloadable PDF reports.
6. **Hindi Support** — Complex concepts explained in Hindi when needed.
